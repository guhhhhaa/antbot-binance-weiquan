Linktree Creator Services Program

We built the Linktree Creator Services Program as a way to empower Linktree’s most impactful creators—both breaking and established—to get as much value from our platform as possible.

 

Through white glove service alongside unique access to data, functionality, and opportunities, we strive to facilitate an experience that cements Linktree as an essential pillar for achieving your digital goals.

Eligibility Requirements

Activation

You have a Linktree and it’s in the bio of your most followed social media account

Engagement

Your Linktree consistently drives a substantial number of profile views

Social Reach

You have a significant following on your primary social media channel

Featured Benefits*

Expedited Support

Your first touchpoint for support is a dedicated member of Linktree’s Creator Partnerships team

Early Feature Access

Get access to certain new Linktree features before anyone else

Strategy and Insights Calls

Regular 1-on-1 check-ins with a dedicated Creator Partnerships team member to review your Linktree performance

First Look for Paid Collabs

We’ll think of you first whenever we’re sourcing talent for brand partnership opportunities

*The specific set of benefits you qualify for will be determined by your engagement and your social reach. Not all creator services participants will qualify for all benefits, but all creator services participants will have the opportunity to expand the benefits they qualify for as they grow.
