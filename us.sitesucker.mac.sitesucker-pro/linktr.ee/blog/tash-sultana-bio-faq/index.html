19 things you didn’t know about Tash Sultana

If you’re a music-head, then you probably love learning about talented new artists making unique music. If you haven’t heard of Tash Sultana, read on to learn more, and then strap on your headphones and start exploring their work. For those of you who are already fans, test your Tash Sultana trivia knowledge with these 19 facts.

Who is Tash Sultana?

Tash Sultana is an Australian multi-hyphenate music performing artist. They are a singer-songwriter, multi-instrumentalist, producer, and engineer with two albums and a series of awards and nominations to their name.

How did Tash Sultana rise to fame?

Sultana started busking and performing at open mics before going viral on YouTube in 2016. Their bedroom recording of the psychedelic-reggae song “Jungle” earned one million YouTube views in just five days and has continued to rack up engagement. They have since dropped two albums, Flow State (2018) and Terra Firma (2021), and performed at dozens of sold-out concerts worldwide.

How does Tash Sultana use Linktree?

Sultana keeps it simple. They use a lavender gradient background with easily accessible link tabs. They share a link to not only their music but also lyrics – although both links direct users to Sultana’s website.

04

What can you find on Tash Sultana’s Linktree?

Sultana’s Linktree features upcoming tour dates, music, lyrics, and more. If you’ve ever wondered where to pick up some merch and prove yourself a true stan, head over to their Linktree. While you’re there, sign up for their mailing list and stay in the know about upcoming music releases and events.

05

What was Tash Sultana’s debut album called?

Sultana’s debut album was 2018’s Flow State, which won the ARIA Award for Best Blues and Roots Album and was nominated for Australian Album of the Year at the 2018 J Awards. It won album of the year in 2019 at the Pop Awards, where Sultana was also named Emerging Artist of the Year.

What is Tash Sultana’s most streamed song?

At the time of writing, Sultana’s top song on Spotify is “Jungle,” with more than 200 million streams. “Notion” comes in second place with around 130 million. These are also their top two songs on Apple Music.

07

What instruments does Tash Sultana play?

Remember when we said Sultana is a multi-hyphenate performer? They play an impressive range of instruments. Sultana plays the piano, keyboard, synth, bass, drums, trumpet, saxophone, flute, mandolin, oud, harmonica, lap steel, and panpipes. They also make and sample beats, beatbox,es and have a 5-octave vocal range.

08

Who is Tash Sultana’s partner?

Searching for info on Sultana’s wife? Although they don’t talk about their love life too often on social media, Sultana got engaged to their long-time partner Jaimie Englewood in 2020.

09

Has Tash Sultana won any awards for their music?

 

Yes, and they’ve received even more nominations. They won Best Blues and Roots Album at the 2018 ARIA Music Awards for Flow State, received accolades at the National Live Music Awards in 2016 and 2017, and clinched a Rolling Stone Australia Award for the song “Pretty Lady” in 2021.

10

What genre of music does Tash Sultana make?

Not only is Sultana a multi-instrumentalist, but their music also spans a broad range of genres. They produce psychedelic rock, reggae, soul, R&B, hip hop, blues, roots, and neo-soul.

11

What is Tash Sultana’s latest album?

Sultana’s second album, Terra Firma, released in 2021, is their most recent at the time of writing. It earned an ARIA Award nomination for Best Soul/R&B Release in 2021 as well as a nomination for Rolling Stone Australia’s Best Record award in 2022.

What label is Tash Sultana signed to?

Sultana presently releases their work through Lonely Lands records, their own record company. Their work is distributed by other labels globally, such as Sony Music.

Where is Tash Sultana from?

Sultana is from Melbourne, Australia. They are of Maltese ancestry.
