17 things you didn’t know about Justin Baldoni

Even if you’re the world’s biggest Jane the Virgin fan, there are probably a few facts about this actor you don’t know. Read the list below to brush up on your knowledge of this famous performer.

Who is Justin Baldoni?

Justin Baldoni is an American actor and filmmaker known for his role as Rafael Solano on the hit series Jane the Virgin.

01

The basics

 

Name: Justin Baldoni
Birthday: January 24, 1984
Nationality: American
Zodiac sign: Aquarius
Linktree username: justinbaldoni

02

What is Justin Baldoni’s full name?

The actor/filmmaker’s full name is Justin Louis Baldoni.

03

What is Justin Baldoni’s Ted Talk about?

Justin Baldoni’s Ted Talk is about redefining masculinity and how men view themselves. He encourages men to go deeper into their feelings, find their vulnerabilities and learn how to communicate them.

04

What can you find on Justin Baldoni’s Linktree?

Justin Baldoni’s Linktree redirects to his Ted Talk on YouTube, “Why I’m Done Trying to Be Man Enough.”

How does Justin Baldoni use Linktree?

Baldoni uses a plain white background with mustard tabs that collectively communicate simplicity. He even uses a “Text me” tab for his followers to directly contact him.

What other passions does Justin Baldoni have besides acting and directing?

As you may have inferred from some of the answers above, Justin Baldoni is an activist. He aims to shatter previous standards of masculinity and empower boys and men to be vulnerable. He practices Bahá’í faith.

07

Does Justin Baldoni have a podcast?

He sure does. Justin Baldoni also talks about masculinity and gender roles on The Man Enough Podcast, which explores what it means to be a man and a human.

09

Who is Justin Baldoni's wife?

 

Baldoni is married to Emily Fuxler (now Baldoni), a Swedish actress. She’s appeared in films like Coherence and Ghosts of Girlfriends Past and TV series like CSI, Bones, Mad Men, and How I Met Your Mother.

10

What did Justin Baldoni aspire to do when he was young?

Baldoni didn’t necessarily want to be a Hollywood star when he was younger. A talented athlete, he ran track and played soccer in high school. He even scored a scholarship to California State University, Long Beach. While in high school, he also worked at a radio station, but according to this piece from Onward State, he didn’t see himself in an entertainment career yet. Later, Baldoni not only starred in a hit series, but he created the most-watched digital documentary series in history, My Last Days, which features stories of people living with terminal diseases.

Meet Christopher, focus on the good | My Last Days

11

What is Justin Baldoni’s book about?

Baldoni has written two books. Man Enough: Undefining My Masculinity (2021) explores the traditional stereotypes about masculinity in relation to vulnerability, relationships, body image, and more. Boys Will Be Human (2022) is a self-help book for young boys that address self-esteem and communication. Fun fact: Justin Baldoni is a New York Times bestselling author.

Where is Justin Baldoni from?

Baldoni was born in Los Angeles, California, but he was raised in Medford, Oregon.

13

Where does Justin Baldoni live?

 

According to LA Travel Magazine, the actor and his wife live in Ojai, California.

14

What was Justin Baldoni’s breakthrough role?

“Breakthrough” may be a subjective term, but Baldoni is well-known for his role in Jane the Virgin. He also cites it as a turning point for him as a person, saying the show taught him about diversity and inclusion.

15

Who are Justin Baldoni’s parents?

Justin Baldoni’s parents are Sharon and Sam Baldoni. His grandfather is Louis Baldoni, a former Indiana senator.

16

Did Justin Baldoni start acting as a teenager?

According to Justin Baldoni’s Wikipedia page, he didn’t start working as an actor until he was about 20, which was in 2004.
