13 things you never knew about Bhad Bhabie

Bhad Bhabie is the kind of internet personality who’s not shy about making a scene. So, even if you’re not following her, you’re probably familiar with her origins and confrontational vibe. But even the biggest Bhad Bhabie fans might not know the following 13 facts.

Who is Bhad Bhabie?

 

Danielle Bregoli (stage name Bhad Bhabie) is a rapper and internet personality. She became famous for a controversial and massively viral interview with Dr. Phil, after which she became known as the “cash me outside” girl.

How did Bhad Bhabie get famous?

Bhad Bhabie, a.k.a. Danielle Bregoli, became famous after appearing in a Dr. Phil segment with her mother, titled “I Want to Give Up My Car-Stealing, Knife-Wielding, Twerking 13-Year-Old Daughter Who Tried to Frame Me for a Crime.” The episode centered on Bregoli’s outrageous behavior and her mother’s concerns about it. When the audience reacted negatively to Bregoli’s disposition, she clapped back, saying “Catch me outside, how ‘bout that?” as if challenging the audience to a fight. It only takes a few words to make someone famous. An affected version of the catchphrase (“Cash me ousside, how ‘bout dah”) went viral, landing everywhere from T-shirts to dance song remixes.

How much is Bhad Bhabie worth?

Bhad Bhabie claims she’s earned more than $50 million on OnlyFans alone. She even showed off screenshots as proof of her earnings on Instagram.

Why did Bhad Bhabie go to Turn-About Ranch?

 

Bregoli went to Turn-About Ranch after her controversial behavior on Dr. Phil. Turn-About Ranch is a center for “troubled teens” in Utah. In a YouTube video, Bregoli talks about her time there and the negative experience she had.

07

How old was Bhad Bhabie when she released her first single?

Bhad Bhabie released her first single, “These Heaux,” when she was only 14 years old.

08

What artists has Bhad Bhabie collaborated with?

 

Bhad Bhabie has released hip-hop tracks with “Hi Bich (remix)” with YBN Nahmir, “Affiliated” with Asian Doll, “Gucci Flip Flops” with Lil Yachty, “Trust Me” with Ty Dolla $ign, “Bestie” with Megan Thee Stallion and “Bestie” with Kodak Black, among others.

09

What’s Bhad Bhabie’s biggest hit to date?

At the time of this article, Bhad Bhabie’s most-streamed song on Spotify is her collaboration with Lil Yachty, “Gucci Flip Flops,” boasting roughly 200 million streams. It’s also her top track on Apple Music, followed by “Bestie,” featuring Kodak Black.

10

Has Bhad Bhabie won any awards?

Bregoli was nominated for a 2018 Billboard Music Award for Top Rap Female Artist.

How old is Bhad Bhabie now?

Born on March 26, 2003, Bhad Bhabie is 19 at the time of writing. She rose to fame very early in her teenage years.

Where is Bhad Bhabie from?

Bregoli was born in Boynton Beach, Florida.

Why are Bhad Bhabie’s nails notorious?

Bregoli is known for her long acrylic nails. According to The New York Times, she gets them done twice a week.
